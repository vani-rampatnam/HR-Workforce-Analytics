# 📊 HR Workforce Analytics Dashboard
An HR Leaderboard 

## 🔍 Project Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing employee workforce data to uncover **attrition trends, workforce distribution, and key HR insights**.  
The goal is to enable **data-driven HR decision-making** and improve employee retention strategies.without digging into multiple reports.


---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyze employee demographics and workforce distribution  
- Identify patterns and drivers of employee attrition  
- Monitor key HR KPIs such as attrition rate, tenure, and salary trends  
- Provide actionable insights to improve workforce planning and retention  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard development & visualization  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning & preprocessing  
  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure  

### 1️⃣ Workforce Overview  
- Total employees  
- Department & role distribution  
- Gender and age segmentation  

### 2️⃣ Attrition Analysis  
- Attrition rate (%)  
- Department-wise attrition  
- Attrition by age group and experience  

### 3️⃣ Salary & Tenure Insights  
- Average salary by role  
- Tenure distribution  
- Attrition vs years at company  

### 4️⃣ Root Cause Indicators  
- Early tenure attrition (<2 years)  
- Department-level attrition concentration  
- Potential drivers impacting employee retention  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  
- Higher attrition observed among employees with **less than 2 years of experience**, indicating onboarding or expectation gaps  
- Certain departments show **disproportionately high attrition**, suggesting workload or management challenges  
- Salary and tenure patterns indicate potential misalignment in compensation and growth opportunities  

---

## 💡 Business Impact  
- Enables HR teams to **identify high-risk attrition segments**  
- Supports **data-driven retention strategies** and workforce planning  
- Helps reduce **hiring and training costs** by improving employee retention  
- Improves overall **employee experience and organizational stability**
